How to Use Rapid Lister Pro

Follow these simple steps to install and start automating your listings.

1

Download & Extract

Click the Download button above to download the `rapid-lister-pro.zip` file. Extract the ZIP folder on your computer.

2

Install in Chrome

Open Chrome and go to chrome://extensions/. Turn on Developer Mode (top right toggle). Click Load unpacked and select the extracted folder.

3

Enter Listing Details

Click the Extension icon on your toolbar. Enter your product Title, Price, Description, Tags, and choose the number of bulk listings you want to create.

4

Start Bulk Lister

Click View Listings. The tool will automatically open background tabs, upload photos, fill details, and save them as drafts.

5

Auto-Publish Drafts

After drafts are created, the tool opens your Facebook Selling Page. Click START AI PUBLISH on the floating control box to publish all items automatically.
